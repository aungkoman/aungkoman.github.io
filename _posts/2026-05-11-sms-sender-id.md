# Sender ID Registration: Fees, Documents, and Terms

## 💰 Fees and Duration

* **Registration Scope:** Your Sender ID will be registered across **all network operators** in Myanmar.
* **Total Fee:** A charge of **MMK 450,000** applies for each Sender ID registration.
* **Service Fee:** SMSPoh does not charge a service fee; the registration is **free** from our side. The total amount is paid directly to the network operators.
* **Processing Time:**
* Standard: **2 to 5 weeks**.
* Delays: May occur due to incomplete documentation or information mismatches.


* **Re-registration:** If switching from another provider, the Sender ID must be re-submitted and registered with SMSPoh.
* **Refund Policy:** Registration fees are **non-refundable**.

---

## 📄 Documents Required

To initiate the process, the following documents are mandatory:

1. **LOA (Letter of Authorization):** Must be on official letterhead that matches the requested Sender ID.
2. **NRC (National Identity Card):** A clear copy of the applicant’s national ID.
3. **MFS License:** Only required if the service involves Mobile Financial Services.
4. **Template Submission:** SMSPoh will prepare and submit message templates to the providers on your behalf based on your registration form.

---

## ⚖️ Terms and Conditions

### Character & Naming Rules

* **Length:** Maximum of **11 characters**.
* **Restrictions:** No special characters; cannot consist solely of English words.
* **Brand Integrity:** A Sender ID must represent a **single brand** only.
* **Localization:** International brands may be required to append **"MM"** if locally registered.
* **Forbidden IDs:** Generic/Shared IDs (e.g., "OTP", "Info") or long-digit numbers (e.g., 09xxxxxxxx) are not allowed.
* **Formatting:** Sender IDs are **case-sensitive** and cannot be changed once approved.

### Usage & Penalties

* **Inactivity:** If inactive for **one year**, the ID will be deactivated by authorities and require re-submission.
* **Prohibited Activities:** Bypassing international routes, financial fraud, spamming, or gambling will result in an immediate block and potential penalty fees.
* **SMSPoh Limitation:** The Service Provider cannot resolve issues with telecom companies arising from illegal activities or policy violations.

---

## 🛡️ Authorization, Ownership, and Liability

### Ownership Warranty

The Client warrants they are the legal owner of the brand or possess written authorization from the owner. All submitted materials (certificates, trademarks, etc.) must be **authentic and valid**.

### Liability & Indemnity

* **Verification:** SMSPoh is not responsible for verifying ultimate ownership beyond the provided documents.
* **Legal Responsibility:** The Client bears full liability for claims involving forged documents, trademark infringement, or unauthorized registration.
* **Suspension Power:** SMSPoh reserves the right to **immediately revoke or suspend** a Sender ID in the event of a dispute or regulatory complaint without prior notice.
* **Indemnification:** The Client agrees to indemnify SMSPoh and telecom partners against all costs, fines, or legal actions resulting from improper usage.

---
