### Advanced: Use Family-Safe DNS (Network-Wide)
If you want to ensure these sites are blocked even if they use a different browser or app, you can change the **Private DNS** on the device:
1.  On the Android device, go to **Settings** > **Network & Internet** > **Private DNS**.
2.  Select **Private DNS provider hostname**.
3.  Enter: `family-filter-dns.cleanbrowsing.org`
4.  **Save.** > [!IMPORTANT]
