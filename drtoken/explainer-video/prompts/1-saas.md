Here is a comprehensive prompt you can use—whether you are feeding it to an AI video generator or handing it off to an animation team as a creative brief.

To make this actionable, the narrative in this prompt is structured around a specialized CRM system handling provisioning and billing for Internet Service Providers (ISPs), but the core structure can be easily adapted to any software.

### **The Animation Prompt**

> **Art Style & Global Directives**
> **Style:** Minimalist 2D vector explainer animation.
> **Characters:** Stick-figure-adjacent anatomy with oversized heads, simple cylindrical limbs, and no intricate physical details (no fingers or toes). Facial features must be strictly minimalist—dot eyes and single-line mouths that change shape to convey emotion.
> **Environment:** Flat, geometric spaces resembling an office or server room. Use a side-scrolling, diorama-like layout with zero 3D perspective depth. Keep backgrounds uncluttered.
> **Colors & Textures:** Flat cell-shading with zero gradients or realistic shadows. Characters and objects must have thick, bold outlines for high contrast. Use a muted, calming color palette for the background, reserving bright, high-contrast accent colors exclusively for digital UI elements.
> **Scene 1: The Bottleneck (0:00 - 0:15)**
> **Setting:** A minimal, geometric office setting.
> **Action:** A frustrated manager sits at a desk. Their simple line-mouth is curved downward. Surrounding them in the physical space are diegetic, floating UI pop-ups representing chaotic spreadsheets, missed billing cycles, and manual network provisioning errors. The digital elements are slightly more detailed than the background, creating visual clutter that overwhelms the character.
> **Scene 2: The Search for a Solution (0:16 - 0:25)**
> **Setting:** Same office, punched in closer on the character.
> **Action:** The manager picks up their phone. A skeuomorphic search bar floats into the air next to their head. They scroll through generic software options that appear inside a large thought bubble. The character shakes their head; the options are clearly too broad and not tailored to their specific industry needs.
> **Scene 3: The Seamless Integration (0:26 - 0:40)**
> **Setting:** A split-screen visual metaphor.
> **Action:** The screen splits down the middle. On the left is the cluttered, chaotic office. On the right, a clean, highly organized SaaS dashboard tailored for ISPs slides into view. The dividing line sweeps across the screen from right to left, wiping away the chaotic floating spreadsheets. The manager's facial expression shifts to a relaxed smile. The floating UI elements elegantly snap into organized, color-coded widgets representing automated billing and instant provisioning.
> **Scene 4: The Call to Action (0:41 - 0:50)**
> **Setting:** A solid, flat-colored background.
> **Action:** The manager character gives a simple thumbs-up. The camera pans quickly to the right, leaving the character behind to center on the SaaS logo, a brief tagline, and the website URL in clean, bold typography.

### **Why This Prompt Works for the Style**

* **Diegetic Tech:** Explicitly asking for UI elements to float in the physical space perfectly mimics the "intrusive social media" effect from your reference video.
* **Emotional Mirroring:** It establishes a clear A-to-B emotional arc (Frustration $\rightarrow$ Relief) using only the simplest geometric line changes on the character's face.
* **Visual Metaphor:** The split-screen wipe specifically requests a cinematic transition that turns a chaotic state into an organized one without needing complex 3D camera moves.
