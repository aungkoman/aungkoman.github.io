
> **Art Style & Global Directives**
> **Style:** Minimalist 2D vector explainer animation.
> **Characters:** Stick-figure-adjacent anatomy with oversized heads, simple cylindrical limbs, and minimal facial features (dot eyes and single-line mouths).
> **Environment:** Flat, geometric hospital/clinic setting with zero 3D perspective depth. Use a side-scrolling, diorama-like layout.
> **Colors & Textures:** Flat cell-shading with zero gradients or realistic shadows. Characters and objects must have thick, bold outlines for high contrast. Muted, calming background colors, with bright accent colors for screens.
> **Scene 1: The Chaotic OPD (0:00 - 0:12)**
> **Setting:** A highly crowded Outpatient Department (OPD) waiting room.
> **Action:** Dozens of stick-figure patients are packed closely together on geometric benches and standing in lines. Their line-mouths are curved downward, showing anxiety and exhaustion. Visual clutter (clocks spinning fast, exclamation marks in thought bubbles) emphasizes the long waiting time.
> **Scene 2: The Solution Appears (0:13 - 0:25)**
> **Setting:** Zoom in on a single patient standing in the crowd.
> **Action:** A clean, flat-styled TV monitor lowers from the top of the screen, clearly displaying a bold "Doctor's Token Number: #24". Simultaneously, the patient looks down at their smartphone. A floating UI bubble pops up from the phone, mirroring the exact same live token status and queue updates. The patient’s expression instantly changes to a relaxed smile.
> **Scene 3: The Transformation (0:26 - 0:38)**
> **Setting:** Pull back to the full waiting area.
> **Action:** A visual "wipe" effect sweeps across the screen. As the wipe passes, the massive crowd disappears, leaving only a few calmly spaced-out patients sitting comfortably. The chaotic, crowded OPD transforms into a serene, organized, and uncrowded environment. Characters are smiling, indicating significantly less waiting time.
> **Scene 4: The Core Message & Call to Action (0:39 - 0:50)**
> **Setting:** A clean, solid-colored background matching the hospital branding.
> **Action:** Elegant, bold typography animation appears in the center of the screen, reading: "Reduce Patient Waiting Time 80%". Below the main tagline, a placeholder for the software logo and a website URL smoothly fades into view.
